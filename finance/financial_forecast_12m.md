## 5. Break-Even Analysis

### Fixed Monthly Costs at Steady State: ~$1,715/mo (M7+)

**Break-even calculation (Pro-only):**
- Gross profit per Pro customer: $49 - $10 = $39/mo
- Break-even: $1,715 / $39 = **44 Pro customers**
- Expected timeline: Month 7-8 (base scenario)

**Break-even calculation (blended with Enterprise):**
- Each Enterprise customer gross profit: $499 - $80 = $419/mo
- 1 Enterprise customer offsets ~11 Pro customers
- With 2 Enterprise + 20 Pro: ($419 x 2) + ($39 x 20) = $1,618/mo (near break-even)
- Expected timeline: Month 5-6 (base scenario)

### Cumulative Break-Even (total revenue = total expenses + initial costs)
- Total Year 1 expenses: ~$12,480 + $195 = **$12,675**
- Conservative: Cumulative revenue exceeds $12,675 around **Month 9-10**
- Base: Cumulative revenue exceeds $12,675 around **Month 7-8**
- Optimistic: Cumulative revenue exceeds $12,675 around **Month 5-6**
