## Implementation Steps

1. **Immediate (Board does)**: Scan/upload Dunford, Cialdini, Ogilvy, Ries/Trout books to NotebookLM
2. **CMO does**: Scrape free articles from andrewchen.com, brianbalfour.com, Andy Raskin's Medium
3. **Query NotebookLM for**:
   - "How should a technical founder position a new infrastructure category to developers?"
   - "Write messaging for CISO buyer personas who need AI agent audit trails"
   - "Create HN launch post structure for a governance framework used by the company selling it"

## Critical Use Cases

- **Launch Blog Post**: Ask NotebookLM to critique drafts against Raskin's strategic narrative framework
- **LinkedIn Posts**: Generate 10 post hooks using Cialdini's persuasion principles + Dunford's positioning
- **White Paper**: Structure enterprise compliance messaging using Moore's chasm-crossing language

## Success Metric

NotebookLM can answer: "How would April Dunford position Y*gov differently than a generic compliance tool?"

**Expected answer**: Focus on operational outcome ("unblock multi-agent deployment") not feature ("audit logs").

---

**Total Setup Time**: ~2 hours (mostly Board scanning books)
**CMO Maintenance**: 30 min/week adding new Lenny/Andrew Chen posts
**ROI**: Avoid generic SaaS messaging, speak directly to "AI agent operator" pain points
