# LinkedIn Strategy Proposal — Y* Bridge Labs

**Objective:** Establish Y* Bridge Labs' LinkedIn presence as the authority on AI agent governance, drive enterprise lead generation, and support the Y*Defuse launch.

**Timeline:** 12-week execution plan starting Week of 2026-04-14

---

## 1. Strategic Foundation

### Why LinkedIn Now

- Enterprise buyers (CTOs, CISOs, compliance officers) live on LinkedIn
- Y*gov's enterprise tier ($499/mo) requires a B2B channel -- LinkedIn is the only platform where decision-makers actively read technical content
- Y*Defuse launch creates a natural content moment to bootstrap the account
- Competitors (Anthropic, OpenAI) post about safety in abstract terms -- we have concrete, deterministic claims with real case evidence

### Account Strategy

**Company page:** Y* Bridge Labs
**Personal brand accounts (higher reach):**
- Haotian Liu (Founder) -- primary thought leadership voice
- AI team members -- amplification and engagement

**Positioning:** "The company that governs AI agents with math, not more AI."
