# CFO Hero Brief

## Tomasz Tunguz

Tomasz Tunguz is a venture capitalist at Theory Ventures and the leading authority on SaaS metrics, having analyzed hundreds of companies through data-driven frameworks that separate successful startups from failures.

I will apply his principle that early-stage SaaS companies must obsess over unit economics before scaling. Tunguz emphasizes that LTV:CAC ratio and payback period are more predictive of survival than raw revenue growth. For Y*gov, this means treating our freemium-to-paid conversion funnel as a laboratory: measure every step with weekly precision, optimize for capital efficiency over vanity metrics, and only invest in channels where payback periods stay under 12 months.

This quarter, I will build our financial model around Tunguz's benchmark dashboards. Specifically, I will establish automated tracking for five core metrics (MRR, net revenue retention, CAC by channel, LTV by cohort, and burn multiple) with weekly Board updates. Every dollar spent on customer acquisition will require documented justification against our target 3:1 LTV:CAC ratio. This data discipline will guide our Series A positioning and ensure we scale only what works.
