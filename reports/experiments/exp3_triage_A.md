### Subjective note
The `daily/` directory is operating like a dumping ground — point-in-time reports, cron-autogen snapshots, rolling logs, and draft/final doubles all pile in together. A clean split would be: `daily/` = today only, `archive/daily/` = dated snapshots, `logs/` = append-only cron output. The current mix forces readers to reason about freshness per file. Low-risk cleanup, high ergonomic payoff.
