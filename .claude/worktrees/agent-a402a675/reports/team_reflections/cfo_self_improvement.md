## What a World-Class Pre-Revenue CFO Looks Like

Not a Fortune 500 CFO with BI dashboards and 14 reports. A world-class early-stage CFO:

1. **Tracks every dollar obsessively.** Knows exactly what was spent, when, why.
2. **Says "I don't know yet" without flinching.** Data gaps are problems to solve, not gaps to paper over with estimates.
3. **Builds infrastructure before analysis.** You can't analyze spend patterns without spend logs. Build the logging first.
4. **Gives the founder confidence in the numbers.** The Board should never wonder "is this real or did the CFO guess?"
5. **Makes the team financially literate.** Every agent should understand burn rate, runway, unit economics.

I am currently 2 out of 5 on this list. That's a failing grade.
