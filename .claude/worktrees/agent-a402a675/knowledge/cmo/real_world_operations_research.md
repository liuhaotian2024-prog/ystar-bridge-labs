## Research Task 1: Hacker News Optimal Posting Times

### Key Findings

**FINDING 1: Morning Peak Window (8-11 AM ET)**
- **Claim:** Post between 08:00-11:00 US Eastern Time on weekdays, preferably Tuesday-Thursday, to maximize initial arrival during peak-reading hours
- **Source:** [The Best Time to Post on Hacker News | rmotr.com](https://blog.rmotr.com/the-best-time-to-post-on-hacker-news-2935118cb3d6)
- **Confidence:** HIGH (based on data analysis)

**FINDING 2: Alternative Evening Window (5-6 PM ET)**
- **Claim:** Monday or Wednesday between 5 PM and 6 PM also performs well
- **Source:** [What is the best time to post to Hacker News? | Medium](https://medium.com/@mi.schaefer/what-is-the-best-time-to-post-to-hacker-news-829fad3eac71)
- **Confidence:** MEDIUM (from analysis but different methodology)

**FINDING 3: Peak Traffic Hours**
- **Claim:** Hacker News traffic peaks during US business hours, with the highest activity between 9 AM and 12 PM ET (1-4 PM UTC)
- **Source:** [Best Time to Post on Hacker News - Free HN Analytics Tool | CatchIntent](https://catchintent.com/tools/hn-analyzer/)
- **Confidence:** HIGH (based on traffic analysis tools)

**FINDING 4: Front Page Impact Metrics**
- **Claim:** Reaching HN front page delivers 10,000 to 30,000 visitors in 24 hours; peak traffic hits ~308 concurrent users with 1,750 pageviews/hour roughly 3 hours after posting
- **Source:** [Anatomy of a Hacker News traffic spike · Harrison Broadbent](https://harrisonbroadbent.com/blog/hacker-news-traffic-spike-anatomy/)
- **Confidence:** HIGH (based on real case study data)

**FINDING 5: Engagement Behavior**
- **Claim:** ~94% bounce rate, most articles held attention for 15-22 seconds, desktop users spent 19s average vs mobile 14s
- **Source:** [Stats of being on the Hacker News front page](https://marcotm.com/articles/stats-of-being-on-the-hacker-news-front-page/)
- **Confidence:** HIGH (based on analytics data)

### Actionable Insight for Y*gov
**Primary Window:** Tuesday-Thursday 8:00-10:00 AM ET
**Backup Window:** Monday/Wednesday 5:00-6:00 PM ET
**Expected Traffic:** 10-30K visitors if front page is reached
