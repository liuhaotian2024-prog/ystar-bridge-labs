## English: "Who Governs the Agents?"

**What we're doing:**
We're building a real company where AI agents do the work and govern themselves using the product we sell. Every decision is audited, tamper-proof, and public on GitHub. Day 11. 806 tests. 38 governance tools. Zero fabrication tolerance.

**Why this matters:**
If you run a team — human or AI — you already know the problem. Someone does something they shouldn't. You find out too late. We built the layer that stops it before it happens.

AI agents are reading your files, running your commands, and making decisions with real consequences. Not after the fact. Not with another AI watching. With deterministic, mathematically verifiable rules that no prompt can bypass. We believe this is a civilizational problem, and we're solving it by eating our own dogfood — running our entire company on the governance system we built.

**What we've proven:**
50 attack variants, 100% blocked. 50 concurrent agents, zero data leaks. 16 internal governance mechanisms verified live — not mocked, not simulated, actually running. The first AI governance system that governs itself.

**What we need:**
